OkeyDokey


Okey dokey is used in the same way as `OK' to show that you agree to something, or that you want to start talking about something else or doing something else.


"okey-dokey" is a colloquial expression used in American English. It is an informal way of saying "okay" or "alright" and is often used to indicate agreement or acceptance. While "okey-dokey" may not be as commonly used as "okay" or "alright," it is still recognized and understood by many Americans


"Okie dokie" is essentially a way of saying "I understand," "I'm on board," or "I agree" in a cheerful or easygoing tone.
